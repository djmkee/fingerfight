Privacy Policy - Finger Fight

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction and Data Controller

Finger Fight ("the App") is provided by DevMikeePR. This policy is designed to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

We **do not** collect or store any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number directly through the App. All data collected and processed is handled by third-party services integrated into the App for the purpose of advertising and transaction processing.

2. Data Collection by Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that collect data:

A. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses **Google AdMob** to display banner ads and interstitial advertisements. AdMob collects and processes data to serve relevant ads and measure their performance.

  • Type of Data Collected: Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID), approximate geographic location (based on IP address), and device usage information (e.g., interaction with ads, device type, operating system).
  • Purpose: To serve, target, and measure the effectiveness of advertisements.

B. In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

The App uses the **Google Play Billing Library** to process all in-app purchases and subscriptions (like "Remove Ads" and "Premium Lifetime").

  • Type of Data Collected: Transaction details, payment information, and purchase history are collected and processed securely by Google Play.
  • Purpose: To facilitate financial transactions and manage user entitlements (e.g., verifying if the user has paid to remove ads).

3. Data Safety and Retention

We rely on the security and privacy controls provided by Google and the Google Play Store infrastructure.

  • Retention: Data collected by third-party providers (AdMob, Google Play Billing) is governed by their respective privacy policies.
